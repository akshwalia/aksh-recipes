Bhindi ki Sabzi

Description

Bhindi ki sabji is a simple, easy, and tasty vegan recipe of a mildly spiced dry okra curry that tastes delish with some Indian breads like roti or phulka. Bhindi sabzi also makes for a comforting meal with dal and rice â€“ a perfect Indian meal accompanied by some mango pickle or raita or salad.

Ingredients

  • Ladies Finger - 15 chopped into thin rounds
  • Oil - 2 tblspn + 1 tblspn
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Mustard Seeds - 1 tsp
  • Onion - 1 chopped finely
  • Tomato - 2 chopped finely
  • Turmeric Powder - 1 tsp
  • Chilli Powder - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 2 tsp
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tblspn oil in a pan, add lady finger and saute till golden brown, drain and set aside.
  2. Now in the same pan, add more oil if needed, add mustard and urad dal and let it sizzle.
  3. Now add onions and cook till golden.
  4. Add in tomatoes and cook till mushy.
  5. Add salt and spice powders and mix well.
  6. Add in the lady finger and mix well.
  7. Serve.
